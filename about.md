**Hi, I'm Isaiah. This is my website, the archive for all my streams of consciousness.**

I'm an English student located in Austin, Texas. I hope to tend and nurture this page as a sort of digital garden housing all the hobby-related experiments I conduct.

I started this blog for three main reasons: As a way to archive my own learning and  progress, create comprehensive and human-friendly guides, and share reviews and opinions that might to an art which thrives on (and arguably only exists through) collaboration and conversation.

My main areas of interest are as follows:
- **Online**: I love all things computing and spent time studying different areas of Computer Science ranging from C/C++, Python, Networking, OSS, Linux, to Homelab projects and Digital Workflow Setups/Productivity
- **Offline**: I am a lover of literature, if that wasn't already apparent. I spend my afternoons promenading through bookstores, reading random pages of books I can't get myself to finish, and playing video games with my wife. I co-parent an English Lop named Mousse and go bouldering whenever I can brave the forearm soreness.
